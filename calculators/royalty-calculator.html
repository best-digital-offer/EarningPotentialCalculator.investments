Understanding Royalty Investments

Royalty investments involve purchasing a share of future revenue streams from intellectual property like music, patents, or books. Key considerations:

  • Music Royalties: Income from song plays, streaming, and licensing
  • Patent Royalties: Payments for use of patented technology
  • Book Royalties: Earnings from book sales and adaptations
  • Royalty Crowdfunding: Platform-based investments in various royalty streams

Note: Royalty investments are typically illiquid and carry higher risk. Returns can be unpredictable and dependent on the success of the underlying intellectual property.
